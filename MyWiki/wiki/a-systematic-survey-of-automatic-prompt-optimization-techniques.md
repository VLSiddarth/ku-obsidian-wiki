## What this is
[[Automatic Prompt Optimization]] (APO) encompasses techniques designed to programmatically improve the performance of [[Large Language Models]] (LLMs) by automatically refining the [[prompts]] provided to them. It emerged as a solution to the growing challenges of manual [[prompt engineering]], which is often complex, labor-intensive, and difficult to maintain given the rapid evolution of LLM capabilities, diverse tasks, and best practices in [[Natural Language Processing]] (NLP). APO seeks to automate the discovery and refinement of effective prompts to elicit desired responses from [[LLMs]].

## Why it matters
APO significantly reduces the reliance on specialized human expertise for designing effective [[prompts]], thereby lowering the barrier to entry for utilizing [[LLMs]] across various applications. By automating the prompt refinement process, APO techniques enable [[LLMs]] to achieve higher accuracy and generate more relevant outputs without extensive manual tuning. This automation ensures that [[LLM]]-based systems can adapt more readily to new tasks and model advancements, leading to more robust, efficient, and scalable [[LLM]] deployments.
