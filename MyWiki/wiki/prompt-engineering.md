## What this is

Prompt engineering is the process of structuring natural language inputs to
produce specified outputs from a generative artificial intelligence (GenAI)
model. Context engineering is the related area of software engineering that
focuses on the management of non-prompt contexts supplied to the GenAI model,
such as metadata, API tools, and tokens.
