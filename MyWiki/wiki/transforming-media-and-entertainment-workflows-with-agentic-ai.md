## What this is

As media industry demands accelerate and audiences expect increasingly
personalized experiences, traditional automation approaches are reaching their
limits. These limitations are driving the adoption of agentic AI-autonomous
software systems that leverage artificial intelligence to reason, plan, and
complete complex tasks with minimal human oversight. This paradigm shift
promises to transform how we create, process, and distribute media content at
scale.
