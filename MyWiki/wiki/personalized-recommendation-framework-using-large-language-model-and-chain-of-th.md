## What this is

Traditional learning methodologies often fall short of accommodating diverse
learner needs and adapting dynamically to individual learning paces and styles.
This limitation underscores the growing need for personalized learning, which
has the potential to significantly improve learning outcomes, foster deeper
engagement, and enhance learner motivation. This study introduces a novel
personalized recommendation framework (PRF) that leverages large language models
(LLMs) and chain-of-thought (CoT)
